Major Accomplishments of Ronald Reagan

Throughout his presidency, Ronald Reagan accomplished many things. Several of those things can be related to modern issues in the world today. This page will explain what he has accomplished.

Reaganomics

"Reaganomics" was the name for the economic polocies that Reagan employed during his presidency. It has been cited by some as the "Most serious attempt to change the course of the U.S. economic policy by any administration since the New Deal." There were Four major objectives: First, to reduce the growth of Government Spending. Second, to reduce the marginal tax rates on income from both labor and capital. Third, to reduce regulation. Lastly, to reduce inflation by controlling the growth of the money supply. The program created an annual increase in federal spending decreases from 4.0 to 2.5, decreased top marginal tax rate from 70% to 28%, And decreased corporate income tax rate from 48% to 34%.

Foreign Policy

Reagan ended the Cold War by reducing our amount of Nuclear Weapons while increasing the Size of our Missile Defence System, by 1991 the size of the “Star Wars" Project had grown large enough to influence the fall of The USSR.

Equal Rights & Rule of Law

Reagan added funding to helping “Dependent Children”, and training people for public sector work, while cutting funds to everything else.

States' Rights

Reagan believed that a big government would hurt our country, so he worked hard to lower the federal government’s power, and raise the states power.

Peace Through Strength

Reagan showed that we as a country had to stand strong. He funded one of the biggest military defence systems of its time, showing that America was very powerful. But, at the same time he kept peace.

Technological Advancement

Support for the Strategic Defence Initiative and NASA through the times when America need technology to protect them and support for their space program.
